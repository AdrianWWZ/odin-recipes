Creamy Gnocchi With Vegetables

This creamy gnocchi with vegetables, made from prepared gnocchi, frozen vegetables, and a quick herbed cheese sauce, is very fast to make and a great addition to meatless Monday. Feel free to add in strips of grilled chicken if you prefer.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 (12 ounce) package prepared gnocchi
  • 8 ounces frozen mixed vegetables, such as Birds Eye® Normandy blend
  • ½ cup evaporated milk
  • 1 tablespoon pesto
  • 2 ounces spreadable cheese, such as alouette® Garlic & Herbs Soft Spreadable Cheese

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add gnocchi and vegetables, and cook until vegetables are tender and gnocchi is golden, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in evaporated milk, pesto, and alouette cheese, and cook until sauce is smooth and evenly coats gnocchi and vegetables, about 5 minutes. Serve immediately.

Nutrition Facts(per serving)
